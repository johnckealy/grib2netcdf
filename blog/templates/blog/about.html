Grib2netCDF

Grib2netCDF is a simple tool used to convert grib files to netCDF format. It's completely free to use. GRIB (GRIdded Binary or General Regularly-distributed Information in Binary form) is a file format commonly used for meteorological data, and is a World Meteorological Organization (WMO) standard. Simply drag and drop a GRIB2 format file, and click Upload.

You can select your desired fields by ticking or unticking the checkboxes. When you're happy, click Create NetCDF file. Do bear in mind that netCDF files are inherent larger than GRIB, so the download may take time.

You can also see a sample grib, which is taken from NOAA's NOMADs system.

That's it! It's a simple tool, and intended to simplify the fields coming from the grib to make for easier access. That said, it is not fully comprehensive, and some of the fields in the GRIB file will not appear in the NetCDF. That's on purpose, the idea is to separate out the most useful fields.
